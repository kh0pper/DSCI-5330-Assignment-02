# **6. Investment Alternatives**

## The Decision:
**Which investment creates more value?**

### Investment A:
- $50 million per year
- 20-year duration
- Total cash: $1,000 million

### Investment B:
- $40 million per year
- 12-year duration  
- Total cash: $480 million

## Analysis Method:
Present Value of Annuity Formula:
**PV = C Ã— [(1 - (1+r)^-n) / r]**

- C = annual cash flow
- r = discount rate
- n = years
